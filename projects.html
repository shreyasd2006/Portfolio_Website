AI Comorbidity Forecasting

An advanced research project utilizing Graph Neural Networks to predict multi-step disease progression patterns in COPD patients, leveraging the comprehensive MIMIC-IV dataset.

Research Objectives:

  • Develop temporal GNN models for disease trajectory prediction
  • Analyze comorbidity patterns in COPD patients
  • Implement multi-step forecasting algorithms
  • Validate models using MIMIC-IV clinical data
  • Generate interpretable results for clinical decision support

Methodology:

Utilizing PyTorch framework with specialized GNN architectures including GAT, GCN, and temporal attention mechanisms for sequential prediction tasks.
